Congratulations to ASLAG Karsten Mungcal for clinching the bronze medal at the Raw Nationals 2024, the premier powerlifting event of the year in the country.   Mungcal, a freshman Information Technology student, proudly represented the City College of San Fernando Pampanga in the Under 93Kg Powerlifter Collegiate Category of the competition held at Robinsons Galleria in Ortigas Avenue, Quezon City.   Despite being a rookie to the sport, with less than a year of training under his belt, Mungcal managed to ascend the podium, earning a bronze medal in his inaugural powerlifting meet.
